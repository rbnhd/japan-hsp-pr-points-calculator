Explanation and Notes

  • 70 points path: To apply under the 3-year route, we must continuously maintain at least 70 points for the full 3 years immediately before the PR application date.
  • 80 points path: To apply under the 1-year route, we must continuously maintain at least 80 points for the full 1 year immediately before the PR application date (for example, from D-1 year through D).
  • Please note that there are alternative paths to obtain permanent residency that do not require points, such as completing a 10-year residency.
  • 1 For information about which universities are considered "excellent" by MoJ, see: Eligible Universities for HSP (Highly Skilled Professionals) Visa in Japan.
  • 2 Designated training means training specified by the Minister of Justice in a public notice; in practice this is qualifying training under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' Innovative Asia program (the reference target listed by MoJ for Bonus Point 12).
  • 3 For foreign work-related qualification see: List of foreign qualifications, awards, etc. that are eligible.
  • 4 Investment Management Business, etc. covers certain financial business categories such as Investment Management Business, Investment Advisory and Agency Business, and Type II Financial Instruments Business. See the official MoJ points calculation table and JETRO's HSP overview.
  • 5 Local-government target organizations are organizations supported under MoJ-recognized local-government measures for accepting highly skilled foreign professionals. See the MoJ page on local-government support measures for highly skilled foreign professionals.
